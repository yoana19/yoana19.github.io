About Me

Hello and welcome!
I'm a Biomedical Engineering student at the University of Groningen with a strong focus on sustainability and advancing healthcare technologies.
My expertise in research, material science, and biomaterials shapes my innovative approach to projects, where I thrive on solving complex challenges. Explore my portfolio to see how I apply deep technical knowledge to real-world applications.

Engineering and Design Projects

DNA Model

I created this interactive DNA model for a biology class in high school. Later selected by the teacher to demonstrate to other students how the DNA nucleobases work and pair up with each other.

Pet Care System

This intelligent pet care system, me and the team named Alfred, provides an easy solution to pet care when the owner is away. It is based on a Raspberry Pi and an Arduino board.
Alfred got us a 2nd place in the Ocado Technology Hackathon in Sofia in 2019.

Accessible Chess

Me and a fellow classmate worked on an inclusive chess board, that is easy to interact with and provides comfort to blind people or individuals with other visual impairment in the game of chess.

Inclusive Mirror

In an intensive 48-hour marathon, our team developed an intelligent mirror able to help people with visual agnosia easily get through a daily challenge.
This project ranked us at the 3rd place at the Sofia University St.Kliment Ohridski Inventive Marathon, 2019.

Smart Hip Implant

Along with fellow colleagues, we developed and carried out a final prototype, finite element analysis (FEM), cost estimation, and manufacturating details of a hip implant. We focused on a smart implant, able to interact with its environment which could also be used as a drug-delivery system in this way.
The implant was developed for a Medical Device Design course in 2022-2023.
